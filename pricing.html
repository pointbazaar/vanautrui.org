How do we handle Payment?
We take 25% of the Project Budget as a Commitment Fee Upfront,
then start work on the Project. If you are satisfied with the final Results, after revisions
and changes have been requested and implemented, you pay the rest of the Budget.

If there is disagreement about the Project
or you are not satisfied, we will try to fix it.
You will not get the initial 25% back.

There is no possibility of doing the final Payment in Parts or over a longer Period of time.
The final payment is due 30 days after the Project has been finished.
If the final Payment is not delivered, no further steps will be taken, we will simply stop doing business with you.
