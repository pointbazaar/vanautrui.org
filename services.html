Business Website

You have a Small Business but no website? a strong web presence can bring in more clients. Today's clients often don't have the patience to call companies about their needs.
They expect to see everything they need on the companies website.
This is especially true if your company has no physical presence in the city, which can often be costly.
Instead these companies rely on moving the point of sale to wherever the customer is at the moment: Walking through the city, seeing an ad, watching a video of their friends wearing your product, or seeing a promotion of your product on youtube.
However these platforms control your content. If you want the customer to see you how you want to be seen and really give them all the information they need, a website is a good choice.

You can also choose a site-builder like wix.com or jimdo.com.
They will however give you only limited control. As soon as you require features such as :
  • Display of live data
    • a live calendar for an event planning agency
    • live display of the menu for a restaurant
    • number of free seats in a restaurant
  • databases, because you have many products
  • Analytics to understand customer behavior.
it is the right time to work with professionals.
